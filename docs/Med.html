Project goal

Design an app for MedD's Health that allows users to easily assess their symptoms and get possible results of their health.

Problem

people with no income have difficult seeking medical facilities everytime they feel sick and need an app to asess their symptom and help them have an undestanding of their condition


Role in Project

UX designer designing an app for MedD’s Health for symptom assessment


Target audience

MedD targets users with no source of income between 18-75


Key challenges or constraints

  1. Money, unemployed adults don't have a source of income to get medical care.
  2. Accessibility, Platforms for health are not equipped with assistive technologies
  3. IA, Text-heavy menus in apps are often difficult to read

Research study details

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was unemployed adults who don’t have any income.

Impact

The users shred that the app helped with knowing their conditions and one even quoted, ‘i came to know that i worry alot when it came to my health’


What i Learned

I learnt that even though the problem i was trying to solve was a big one diligently going through each step of the process help me come up with solutions that were both useful and feasible
