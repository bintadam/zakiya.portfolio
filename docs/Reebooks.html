Project goal

Designing an app for Reebooks's Bookstore from reservation to obtaining them.


Problem

Design an app for Reebook's Bookstore that allows users to easily reserve their books and get quickest way possible.


Role in Project

UX designer designing an app for Reebooks's Bookstore from reservation to obtaining them.


Target audience

Reebook’s Bookstore targets users who love to read and reserve books between 20-65


Key challenges or constraints

  1. time, old people mostly find no time to look and sho for bookss..
  2. Accessibility, Platforms for shopping are not equipped with assistive technologies
  3. IA, Text-heavy menus in apps are often difficult to read

Research study details

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was unemployed adults who don't have time to go to bookstores and reserve books.

Impact

The users shared that the app helped with reserving the book they wanted and come to get it when the user is free


What i Learned
